#### **Project Manager**

**Responsibilities:**

- **Project Planning & Execution**: The Project Manager is responsible for defining project goals, scope, and deliverables. They create detailed project plans, set timelines, and allocate resources to ensure successful project execution.
- **Team Coordination**: Works closely with development, design, and other departments to ensure the project stays on track. They ensure that all team members understand their roles and the project's requirements.
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Maintains clear communication with both internal and external stakeholders, providing regular updates on project progress, risks, and changes.
- **Risk Management**: Proactively identifies potential risks to the project timeline, scope, or quality and implements strategies to mitigate these risks.

- **Problem-Solving**: Actively removes roadblocks, manages conflicts, and ensures the project progresses without significant delays.
