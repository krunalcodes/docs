**Skills and Experience:**

- **Technical Expertise**: Strong knowledge of at least one of the following technologies:
  - **Node.js** (Express, NestJS)
  - **React** and **Next.js**
  - **React Native**
  - **Laravel**
- **Experience**: Minimum of **2 years** in software development.
- **Leadership**: Leadership skills are desirable but not required.
- **Agile Methodologies**: Experience working in Agile environments (preferably Scrum).

---

**Soft Skills:**

- **Problem Solving**: Ability to work through complex problems and develop effective solutions.
- **Communication**: Strong communication skills to collaborate with team members and stakeholders.
- **Responsibility**: Takes ownership of assigned tasks and delivers quality results.
- **Proactive**: Self-motivated to identify and address issues before they escalate.
- **Collaboration**: Works well in a team environment, actively contributing to team goals.

---

**Cultural Fit:**

- **Proactive and Solution-Oriented**: Takes initiative and finds ways to improve processes and outcomes.
- **Team Player**: Works collaboratively with others, with a focus on achieving team success.
- **Adaptable**: Willing to learn new skills and adapt to evolving project needs.
